How It Works

Select a PDF, choose an annotation type, set the page and position, then download a new annotated copy.

  1. Select one PDF file.
  2. Choose text, highlight, rectangle, or drawing.
  3. Add the annotation and download.

Common Use Cases

Mark review notes, point out fields, highlight important areas, add quick comments, or draw attention to document details.

Privacy

The selected PDF and annotation data stay in your browser. PDFNest does not upload or store your documents.

Limitations

This is a simple browser annotation tool. It places visible marks on the page rather than providing a full multi-page visual editor.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use highlights for existing text and notes for short comments.
  • Choose colors with enough contrast to stay readable.
  • Flatten the PDF afterward if you want annotations to be harder to change.

Before You Download

Preview the annotated page and make sure marks do not hide signatures, totals, or important form fields.

Privacy and Ads

The tool runs in your browser and keeps document processing separate from advertising areas. PDFNest does not send selected document contents to an ad server, and ad containers should stay away from upload, action, and download controls.

FAQ

Can I choose where the annotation goes?

Yes. Use the page number, position, width, and height controls to place the mark.

Can I flatten after annotating?

Yes. Use the Flatten PDF tool after annotation if you want a visible-page copy that is harder to edit.
