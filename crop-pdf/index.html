How It Works

Select a PDF, choose all pages or a page range, set top, right, bottom, and left margins, then download a cropped copy.

  1. Select one PDF file.
  2. Choose pages and crop margins.
  3. Crop and download the new PDF.

Common Use Cases

Trim white margins, prepare pages for printing, remove extra scanner borders, or focus a PDF page on the useful document area.

Privacy

PDF cropping runs locally in your browser. Your selected PDF is not uploaded, stored, or transmitted by PDFNest.

Limitations

This tool sets the visible crop area. Some PDF editors may still be able to access content outside the crop box.

Tips for Best Results

  • Start with small crop values so you do not remove important page content.
  • Use preview to check pages with headers, footers, stamps, or signatures.
  • Apply crop to selected pages when only some pages have large margins.

Before You Download

Review the cropped result page by page because excessive cropping can remove text or page numbers.

Privacy and Ads

The tool runs in your browser and keeps document processing separate from advertising areas. PDFNest does not send selected document contents to an ad server, and ad containers should stay away from upload, action, and download controls.

FAQ

Can I crop different pages differently?

The current tool applies one crop setting to all selected pages.

Does cropping reduce file size?

Not always. Cropping changes the visible page area and may not remove hidden content from the file.
