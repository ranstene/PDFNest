How It Works

Open the watermark tool, select a PDF, enter the text you want to stamp, then choose placement, opacity, and font size. PDFNest creates a new PDF copy with the watermark applied to each page.

  1. Select one PDF file.
  2. Enter watermark text and choose style settings.
  3. Download the watermarked PDF copy.

Common Use Cases

Add labels such as Confidential, Draft, Sample, Approved, Internal Use, or a company name before sharing a document.

Privacy

Your PDF is read and stamped inside the browser. It is not uploaded to a server, and the output file is generated as a temporary local download.

Limitations

This tool adds text watermarks only. It does not currently support image watermarks, per-page custom text, or editing encrypted PDFs without an unlocked copy.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use short watermark text such as Draft, Confidential, or Review Copy.
  • Lower opacity keeps the document readable while still marking the page.
  • Preview placement before downloading, especially on forms with dense text.

Before You Download

Review page visibility and watermark position so the mark does not hide important document details.

Privacy and Ads

The tool runs in your browser and keeps document processing separate from advertising areas. PDFNest does not send selected document contents to an ad server, and ad containers should stay away from upload, action, and download controls.

FAQ

Does the Watermark PDF tool upload my file?

No. PDFNest adds watermarks locally in your browser and does not upload your PDF to a server.

Can I change the watermark style?

Yes. You can change the watermark text, position, font size, and opacity before downloading the PDF.
