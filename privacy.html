Overview

PDFNest provides browser-based document utilities such as merging PDFs, splitting PDFs, signing PDFs, editing PDF metadata, watermarking PDFs, adding page numbers, converting files to PDF, and exporting PDF pages as images. This Privacy Policy explains what happens when you use the site.

Files Stay in Your Browser

Selected PDFs, images, Word documents, spreadsheets, and text files are processed locally by JavaScript running in your browser. The tool does not upload your selected files to a server for processing.

No Document Storage

The site does not store your documents, generated PDFs, exported images, or file contents on a server. Download links are temporary browser object URLs created on your device. They are cleared when you refresh or close the page.

Local Browser Data

The site may use browser features such as temporary memory, local storage, and service worker caching. These features help remember display preferences, keep local app files available offline, and make the tool faster. They are not used to store the contents of your uploaded documents.

Advertising and Cookies

PDFNest includes reserved advertisement areas that may be used with Google AdSense or another advertising provider. Ad providers may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and personalize ads where permitted by law and your settings.

PDFNest does not send the contents of selected PDFs, images, Word files, spreadsheets, or text files to advertising providers for ad targeting. Document processing happens in your browser, and ad containers are separate from upload, tool action, and download controls.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. If Google ads are enabled, you can manage ad personalization through your Google account ad settings and other privacy controls provided by Google.

Consent and Regional Requirements

If advertising, analytics, or cookie-based features are enabled for visitors in regions that require consent, PDFNest should show a consent notice before non-essential cookies are used. The site owner is responsible for configuring any ad or consent tools according to the laws that apply to the site and its visitors.

Analytics

The current version does not require analytics to process files. If privacy-friendly analytics are added later, this policy should be updated to describe what is collected and why.

Third-Party Libraries

The tool uses local JavaScript libraries for PDF handling, ZIP reading, and previews. These library files are served with the site and run in your browser.

Children's Privacy

This tool is intended for general document utility use. It is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the site changes, especially if ads, analytics, contact forms, consent tools, or server-side features are added.
